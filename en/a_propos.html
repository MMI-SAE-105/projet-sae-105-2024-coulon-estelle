About

Created this year, the Sci-fi'stival celebrates science fiction in all its forms. It is the perfect opportunity to meet directors, writers, and artists, discover their works, and interact with them. Since its inception, the festival has quickly established itself as a major event for fans of the genre. For more info, you can check out our weekend program!

My name is Estelle COULON, I am a first-year student at the IUT of Montbéliard in MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). This website was created as part of an individual project on the theme of the prehistory of the future. I chose to work on sunglasses. Indeed, fashion interests me a lot (having a professional baccalaureate in Fashion and Clothing). I hope my website has inspired and informed you.
