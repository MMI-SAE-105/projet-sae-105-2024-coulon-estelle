Behind the Sunglasses

Reflections on Perception and Reality

“Your head will collapse If there's nothing in it And you'll ask yourself Where is my mind?”

“Your head will collapse If there's nothing in it And you'll ask yourself Where is my mind?”

Pixies - Where is my mind? Surfer Rosa

Sunglasses, by hiding the eyes, create a barrier between the wearer and the outside world, adding a layer of mystery and distance. Similarly, “Where Is My Mind?” creates an atmosphere of detachment and introspection, inviting the listener to reflect on their own perception of reality and the illusions that can influence our view of the world.

Cassette “Where is my mind” - The Pixies

Together, these elements highlight the importance of perception and how we choose to see and interpret our environment. They remind us that our view of the world can be influenced by internal and external factors, and that sometimes, it is necessary to question our own perceptions to find a deeper meaning.
